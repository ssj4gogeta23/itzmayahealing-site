About

A simple explanation of who I am and how I work.

My name is Miguel

I approach this work with respect and seriousness. I’m not interested in hype or spiritual theater. I care about what actually changes in a person’s body, mind, and day‑to‑day life.

My style is calm, focused, and protective of your pace. I don’t push people into intensity. I create stability, then let the system respond.

My approach

  • Grounding before anything else
  • Clear boundaries
  • Presence and pacing
  • Simple follow‑up practices when helpful

If you’re sensitive, exhausted, or carrying stress that feels older than the moment, this work can be a steady place to return to yourself.

If you’re curious, start small.
